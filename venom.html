17 september 2020 by Jishnu k

In this standalone Spider-Man spin-off, gonzo journalist Eddie Brock (Tom Hardy) is infected by an extraterrestrial parasite that gives him superpowers while on a mission to expose Riz Ahmed’s corrupt scientist. A nerd with a God complex (as signalled by his turtleneck sweater, zipped to the throat), Ahmed’s Drake has been covertly unleashing alien “symbiotes” on unsuspecting human hosts. Brock, on the other hand, waters his plants, gives money to homeless people and wears a leather bomber jacket. The twist is that the parasite has a personality: “a loser” on his home planet, Venom can communicate with Brock once the two have fused, with a Batman-deep voice and a smiling, malevolent face that looks as though the Scream mask had a baby with Jack Nicholson’s Joker
